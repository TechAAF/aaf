Online Surverys and Reports

Even though we can make a huge difference if we try to, in some situations we cannot really take any action but what we can do is gather as much information as we can and spread awareness amongst people. So, as a part of this initiative, we conduct surveys on topics which are crucial for the society and where people can voice their views without the fear of being exposed. After the surveys, we formulate proper reports to analysis the gathered data and the current situation of our society and then some initiatives that could be taken by the government and the people to improve the situation.

Menstrual Hygiene

Menstrual hygiene is not something that can be taken lightly. It is a grave issue that causes various health problems to thousands of girls every year. Be it a rural area or an urban, it is still seen as a taboo by millions of people and the women are outcasted and treated as degenerates. By conducting this survey we aim to gather information on the level of basic knowledge about menstrual hygiene amongst the people of our country, old and young. We hope to spread some awareness and make people realise that it’s time we let go of these prejudiced old traditions and rituals and accept menstruation as a healthy biological process that is completely normal and in fact, necessary for a healthy functioning of the female body. LGBTQ Community Report

Gender Discrimination means that men and women are not treated equally in our society and they are treated on the basis of their gender . The difference in treatment arises from various cultural and social factors. It is most common in India and is experienced differently across different cultures. By conducting this survey , we aim to gather information on the knowledge level of the individuals of our country in respect of Gender Discrimination. Our basic purpose is to know the viewpoints of different individuals and to spread awareness about the same. The most important reason is to spread and promote Gender equality and to study the prevailing problem in India. LGBT Community is referred to as the gay community comprising of lesbians , bisexual , gay , transgender etc. They are formed by common culture. It is very diverse and they together celebrate pride and individuality . By conducting this survey , we aim to gather information on the level of knowledge of individuals on LGBT community . We aim and hope to spread some awareness and help in making people understand that it's time to accept and respect LGBT community and their rights and culture.

Yoga Report

Yoga Day is celebrated every year on 21st June. The Prime Minister , Narendra Modi suggested and established the international yoga day on 21st June and it is very significant in many parts of the world. Yoga is basically a combination if physical, mental , and spiritual practices largely comprising of postures/ asanas. Yoga is very beneficial to our lives. By conducting this survey, we aim to gather information about the opinion and views of different individuals on yoga and their yoga routine / lifestyle. The main aim was to spread awareness about yoga and making people understand the benefits of it.

Survey About People's View and Knowledge of the Condition of Farmers in India

Farmers, the primary sector responsible for the growth of any economy, are sadly the ones who are mistreated and exploited the most. It is because of them we have food in our homes and the economy has goods to export but do we stop to think and care about them? Over the past decade, an average of 15,000 farmers commit suicide every year. At this rate, soon there will be no farmers left in our country. This survey aims to gather information and spread awareness about the way farmers are exploited and extorted.

Survey on Social Media Cyber Bullying and Opinion of People on the Same.

Social media is basically a technology or a platform to help user or individuals share information , interact amongst others and many other forms of networking . It facilitates the development of social networks by connecting different users together. By conducting this survey, we aim to spread awareness about the importance of social media and in return discussing about Cyberbulling. Our basic purpose was to gain information on the level of social media usage and to address the problems associated with it. In addition to this , our motive was also to gain the information on the opinion of people in respect of cyber bullying and hacking .

Survey about People’s View and Knowledge About Free Metro and DTC Rides for Women in Delhi

Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) government, a 50 per cent equity partner in Delhi Metro along with the centre, is planning to make travelling for women by buses and metro free of cost. As soon as this proposal by the government was released there came forward many views and opinions regarding it. It became a “Hot topic” of discussion. This survey aims to gather information from the general public about their views and opinions regarding the same.

Water | Can we afford to treat it like a necessity?

Do you have enough water for all your needs? is the water you use clean and pure? Do you know that the rural population suffers from low water quality but the urban and semi-urban areas are most prone to water shortage. The water quality needs to be safe and sound at the microbiological level and mere continuous supply is not enough. We at ASHA AKANKSHA FOUNDATION through this survey aims at gaining the knowledge and creating awareness about the present scenario of the availability of water resources.
